What is depicture?

depicture is a game you play online with your friends, with gameplay similar to Scrawl, Sketchy Stories, Drawphone, Drawception, and Telestrations.

The project is open-source - in fact, depicture is dedicated to the public domain, so you are free to do whatever the h*ck you want with the code and assets! Find them here!
